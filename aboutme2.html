Hour of Code

For the Hour of Code event, we started our arrangements by deciding on a school which would be appropriate for the given instructions. Several schools were contacted; however, it became apparent that majority of them either require additional documents or do not give permission for such events at all. Finally, after a few unsuccessful tries, school No. 283 gave us their consent allowing us to hold the event and became our ultimate choice. After completing the most important step, we registered our school's name on the Hour of Code website. The next step for us was to develop a presentation: provide a short introduction to the ADA University and SITE, continuing on with the tutorial for the Classic Maze puzzle. Our preparations are still in progress leaving us halfway ready for the Hour of Code event.
